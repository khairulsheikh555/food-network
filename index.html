About Us

Food Project is the second brand launched in Bangladesh. After months of R&D and data-driven experiments, Food Project developed a menu that was ready to rock the city. Currently operating in Tristate & it’s surrounding areas, Food Project is on its way to establishing itself as the most popular food brand in the city.
